Initial quest
-------------

Solarus Quest Editor provides assets (images, sounds and scripts) when
creating a new quest (File > New quest).
They are located in the directory assets/initial_quest.

- Lua scripts are licensed under the terms of the GNU General Public License
in version 3.

- All data files other than Lua scripts are licensed under
Creative Commons Attribution-ShareAlike 4.0 (CC BY-SA 4.0).
http://creativecommons.org/licenses/by-sa/4.0/

All data files of these assets come from the official
Solarus Free Resource Pack:
https://github.com/solarus-games/solarus-free-resource-pack
